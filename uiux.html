E-receipt

This is an app that I have created with my friend which was "E-receipt". This app allows people to store their receipts online. It is for people who want to keep track of their expenses and receipts.

It is a pain for society nowadays to store receipts in their wallets or files at home. Why store it physically when you can store it digitally. It is much more convenient for people nowadays to take out their phones and be able to do everything with them. With that concept in mind, we decided to create an application that can let users keep track of their receipts and warranties in an app.
